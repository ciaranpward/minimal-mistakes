The purpose of this school is to bring together and build a community collectively interested in solving the electronic structure problem with quantum computers.

This will be a five day event hosted at Newnham College in Cambridge, UK between Monday September 14 and Friday September 18.

This event is being run by Quantinuum and is sponsored by the National Quantum Computing Centre.
