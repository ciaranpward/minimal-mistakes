    About

    About InQuanto School

    InQuanto School 2026 is a summer school focused on quantum computational chemistry, bringing together participants who want a practical introduction to the field and the tools used to study it.

    The programme is designed to balance:

    • foundational teaching on the core ideas
    • guided practical sessions
    • time for questions, discussion, and collaboration

    What To Expect

    Across the event, participants can expect a mix of lectures, interactive sessions, and opportunities to connect with other attendees working in adjacent areas of chemistry and quantum computing.

    The aim is to create a welcoming environment where attendees can build understanding quickly and leave with a stronger sense of how these methods are used in practice.
